Joseph Vijay

Actor|Singer|Politician

Vijay, born Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, India, is a renowned actor and playback singer in the Tamil film industry. Known simply as Vijay, he began his acting career as a child artist and gained prominence in the 1990s. His breakthrough came with the film "Poove Unakkaga" (1996), which established him as a leading actor in Tamil cinema. Vijay has since starred in numerous successful films, showcasing his versatility in roles ranging from action-packed thrillers to heartfelt dramas. He is celebrated for his charismatic screen presence, dance skills, and ability to connect with audiences across different demographics. Some of his notable movies include "Ghilli" (2004), "Thuppakki" (2012), "Mersal" (2017), and "Master" (2021), all of which have been major box-office successes. Apart from acting, Vijay is actively involved in philanthropic activities through his fan clubs and supports various social causes. He is also a talented playback singer, having lent his voice to several songs in his films. Vijay's contributions to Tamil cinema have earned him a dedicated fan base globally, making him one of the most influential and bankable stars in Indian cinema today.
